SMS Donations — Social Impact
250+ downloads 5★ reviews Adopted by nonprofit

About me

The developer behind the apps

I'm Rodrigo, a 36-year-old Android developer from Uruguay. I've been working with Android for over 4 years, and started publishing my own apps on Google Play 2 years ago — always solo, always independently.

My approach is simple: find a real problem, build a clean solution, and ship it. No venture capital, no growth hacks. Just honest software that people actually use every day.

My apps have users in Spain, Mexico, USA, Argentina, and Uruguay. I build in public on X, sharing real numbers, real struggles, and real lessons from the indie dev journey.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me at the gym, running, playing video games, or figuring out the next app to ship. Healthy life, clear mind.
